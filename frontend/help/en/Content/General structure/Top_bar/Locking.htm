Locking and unlocking

It is possible to lock and unlock the WebApp configuration. This prevents unwanted modifications.

When you open the WebApp, the configuration is unlocked. The padlock symbol is closed. This means that no user can edit the configuration.

You need to lock it so that only you can edit the configuration. You can see the current status of the WebApp in the top bar.

 

Locking the configuration

  1. Click on the lock symbol in the top bar.
  2. A pop-up appears.
  3. Enter a lock description.
  4. Click on the button "Submit".
  5. The configuration is now locked for other users. Only you can now make changes.

Unlocking the configuration

  1. Click on the padlock symbol in the top bar.
  2. The configuration is now unlocked. You cannot make anymore changes.

Taking over the lock

If another user has locked the configuration, you can take over their lock.

  1. Click on the lock symbol in the top bar.
  2. Enter a lock description.
  3. Click on the button "Submit".

The current lock owner is notified that you want to take over the lock. The lock owner has 30 seconds to decide whether they want to accept or refuse your takeover. After 30 seconds of inactivity, the lock is automatically transferred to you.
