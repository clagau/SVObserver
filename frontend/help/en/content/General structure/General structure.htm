General structure

Hover over image to enlarge

 

The SVOWebApp user interface is organized into four main sections:

  1. Top Bar
  2. View Set
  3. Bottom bar: View Set Navigation
  4. Bottom bar: Menu

 

Show and Hide the Top or Bottom Bar

You can choose whether you want to see the top or bottom bar in the browser.

Hide the Top or Bottom Bar

You can hide the top bar and/or the bottom bar to allow for more screen space. The above picture shows the hide button within the top bar. You find the same button in the center of the bottom bar.

  1. Mouse over the semi-circular white shape on the bar

    — The button Hide appears

  2. Click on the button Hide

    — The bar disappears and is not anymore shown in the user interface until you choose to view it when required
Display the Hidden Top or Bottom Bar

  1. Mouse over the semi-circular white shape at the center top of the screen to show the top bar respectively at the center bottom to show the bottom bar.

    — The button Show appears

  2. Click on the button Show

    — The bar shows on the screen
